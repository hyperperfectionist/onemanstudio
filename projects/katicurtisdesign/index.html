I crafted a concept site for Kati Curtis Design—known for soulful, layered interiors with global flair. The aim was to reflect her storytelling style and refined personality, using clean design and editorial pacing to bring her portfolio to life.

I framed the structure to feel welcoming and elevated: a hero header with lush visuals, followed by featured projects, service tiles, and testimonials. Each section flows visually—guiding visitors from inspiration to inquiry. The tone feels personal and warm, but also polished and reassuring.
