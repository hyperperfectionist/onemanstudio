This was a conceptual web design for Coral Santoro, crafted to match her forward-facing blend of strategy, tech, and self-expression. I focused on a sharp but fluid layout that supports her multi-hyphenate brand identity and community-first message.

The challenge was to unify Coral’s layered presence—educator, creator, and founder—into one intuitive experience. I leaned into modular structure, high-contrast type, and editorial rhythm to mirror her clarity, confidence, and digital depth.
